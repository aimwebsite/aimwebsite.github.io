OUR STORY

Art in Motion formed through our team's desire to spread the love of dance in our community. In the MVLA school district, dance is spread all across the campus with over 6 classes and 200 students dancing per year. Coming back to dance in person in 2022 made us realize how much of a positive impact dance had in our day to day lives. We became more active, happier, and strongly bonded in our shared love for dance throughout the year; and we hope we can share that love to everyone, starting with our local elementary schoolers.
