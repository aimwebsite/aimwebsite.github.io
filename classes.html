MONDAY

4-5PM

BEGINNER JAZZ

Teachers: Morgan White and Kiana Chen

Description: For all students, recommended for those with little to no jazz experience. Students will learn jazz technique, improve flexibility, and learn choreography.

4-5PM

BEGINNER HIP HOP

Teachers: Liana Qizilbash and Maisie Rennie

Description: For all students, recommended for those with little to no hip hop experience. Students will learn the basics of hip hop movement and technique, as well as learn choreography.

THURSDAY

4-5PM

INTERMEDIATE HIP HOP

Teachers: Sophia Rother and Roya Bahman

Description: Recommended for students who have 1 year of jazz experience. Students will learn higher levels of jazz movement, technique, and dynamic choreography.

4-5PM

INTERMEDIATE HIP HOP

Teachers: Mia Florendo and Avery Olmstead

Description: Recommended for students who have 1 year of hip hop experience. Students will learn the basics of hip hop movement and technique, learn choreography, and develop skills to pick up choreography faster.
