LOCAL DANCE CLASSES FOR ELEMENTARY SCHOOLERS

Here at Art in Motion, we provide affordable dance classes for children of grades 1-8.

All of our profits go to Hope's Corner, a local non profit organization who serves free food and provides necessary resources for those who are homeless.

Our classes include Beginner/intermediate jazz, Beginner/intermediate hiphop, and a stretch and strengthen class to increase strength, flexibility and stamina that will further their training in dance.

Each class will be $12 and our mode of payment will be cash. Our classes are held at cooper park on the concrete space next to the playground.
